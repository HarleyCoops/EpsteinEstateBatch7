The Star Singers Urkant, Ellen, Dagr, and An put on white robes and carried a star for the one with the golden circlet in her hair, and one for the herald. Right after that, the garden was sent for, and as the curtain drew back, I followed with “Glory to God in the highest.” We had to watch every scene within eight minutes, so we had a bit of a dance each time. Then came the Annunciation with Mother and Sybille as Gabriel. I had made a foil for Mother, and she wore a little Christ-child gown and had a little star on the gown underneath. The inn scene: Joe as the innkeeper with an apple, potatoes, and my Vera as the mean innkeeper’s wife, and Sybille as Joseph climbed onto a green cloth on the floor, wrapped in a gray one, with a beard and a staff.
