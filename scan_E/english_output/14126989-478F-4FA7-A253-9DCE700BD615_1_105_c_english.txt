Now that it’s Health Week, there’s a lot of focus again on making sure everyone knows about vitamins and calories. I must say, they pay much more attention to that sort of thing here. People eat a great many fresh apples and oranges, and also far more fresh vegetables than in Germany, which can be bought at low prices. Salads are eaten much more often, especially coleslaw and lettuce, as well as raw carrots (just whole like that), raw celery, raw spinach (very good), radishes, sliced cucumbers (plain), and bell peppers. In fact, salad here is mostly eaten by hand, and there’s something fresh with every meal.
