Dondo, Lindi District, July 27, '66

Dear Mother,

Your letter arrived today with the one from Uncle Max—thank you so much for it, and for your other letter as well. No, I didn't mean to let four or five days go by. The cards were included too. Theklutd's secretaries very often get money or clothes. John has all of that to spare, and it still fits perfectly. Aside from his apprenticeship, he gets £45-50 per week, which is a good 800 DM a month.

You know, Angel, here it's rare that a girl is truly interested and takes on responsibility—I see it with myself at the hospital. My supervisor has been at the hospital for 6 years (3½ years here and 2½ years at another hospital) and often asks me in the evenings if she can go on rounds, and I'll get everything ready.
