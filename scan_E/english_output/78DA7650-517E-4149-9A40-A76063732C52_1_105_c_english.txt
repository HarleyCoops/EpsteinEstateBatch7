Night school starts up again on January 4th; we had two weeks off. Anyway, the big vacation is coming up soon, ten weeks long. Half of all the school children go to work then, in factories or in hospitals as waitresses, which is why the factories and hospitals really go downhill, and other places up there too. Yes, and she is in high school there. And at the university, you choose your own subjects, especially after the first year. History is a required subject, and then you add a certain number of others. The kids choose electives, for instance: Physics, Typing, Bookkeeping, Home Economics. For the boys, there are practical classes too, like lathe work. Everyone does other kinds of work as well. If you don't pass a class, you have to take that subject again along with everything else.
