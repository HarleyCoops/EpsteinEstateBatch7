Toronto, October 10, 1955

Dear Mother,

The "Gotteskind" script arrived the day before yesterday. Thank you so much for it. I'm sorry you had such a hassle with it. But we got right to work today copying out the parts; I got three boys to help me. For now, we've assigned the roles as follows: (God the Father: André); (Angel Gabriel: Erika); (Joseph: Sigismund); (Mary: Michi); (Guldinsach: Uli); (Innkeeper's Wife: Vera); (Herod: Manfred); (Death: Werner); (Devil: Wolf); (Rabant: Horst Wermescher); (Michel: Horst Mielde); (Stoffel: Siegfried); (Cyrich: Werner Kjar); (Melchior: Hans Dieter); (Balthasar: Heinz); (Caspar: Reinhold). We're not yet sure about the Star Singers. I'm not acting in it, since I have to direct and there are more girls than parts anyway—at most, I might play the first Star Singer. The first rehearsal is next Sunday. Inge is going to make the costumes. We do have some good musicians in the group, though. Manfred plays piano well, and so does Hans-Dieter. Klaus (Inge's husband) plays the violin very well, as does Ellen. Werner plays the mandolin, and Reinhold plays the recorder in addition to the accordion. André also plays the piano very well.
