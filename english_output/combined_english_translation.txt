Dear Mother!
How are you? I just wanted to quickly
write to you about the apple prices
at the wholesale market in
Frankfurt. We sold Cox apples
for 48 and 50 Pfennig per pound
to small retailers; there
are still some wormy ones among them.
The large ones, however, are very
nice. Goldparmäne apples are selling
slowly, 25 Pf, but some
are worse than A. There are also
some for 32 Pf. Boskoops only cost
20 to 25 Pf, but they are not
so particularly nice. We aren't selling
any Boskoops yet. In Frank-
furt in the shops, Cox apples are
sold for 70 Pf.
