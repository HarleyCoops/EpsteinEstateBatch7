I think we had 6-8 rowing boats and a motorboat. It was also already possible to swim. There was just one big drawback, the mosquitoes. Mechi got badly bitten, as did everyone who hadn't already taken a trip to the lakes. There was one person with us, Heinz, who was fishing; blood ran down his face, no exaggeration. Mechi was still swollen days later and scratched herself like crazy. However, it had been announced on the radio beforehand that one should be careful and on no account without protective oil (that's what they call it, but boys, it smells even worse than lamp oil from kitchens). We took many pictures; I took proper photos for the first time with the new camera, so I focused mainly on landscapes, apart from the titled report "Water Battle", which consists of twelve consecutive pictures of a battle.
