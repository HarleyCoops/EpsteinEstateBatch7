They are not familiar with that. – Following the celebration, I drove home with the family, so that my Miss O'Connor also came. We, a few girlfriends and acquaintances, later bowled and played. Dr. Harmuth was there, all adults, and two Germans. The father is a farmer and doesn't live in Toronto; the two sisters have a wonderful export business. There are 12 children, imagine that; one of the girls is a nurse, i.e., a Red Cross nurse in the hospitals of Korea, which is also a fulfilling profession. – I came home at 9 PM. I always enjoy it when I'm in homeopathic company, especially with Miss O'Connor; she's a truly fabulous woman. I estimate she's about 33 years old, is terribly fond of the three flowers, and she also says that she often misses the countryside, as she grew up on the farm. I believe I mostly like staying at the hospital when on assignment. For she told the Sister, who has all nurses under her, that she would have liked to be a nurse best, and imagine...
