I waited for it for 2 weeks, and before yesterday I then took the liberty of looking through and reading Mechi's letter, you may think what you want. After all, I wanted to know if the package had arrived at all or not. That we wouldn't know each other very well, I half expected, but that you think that way about me, that appalled me. Then for the first time I regretted that Mechi came over, because she managed that with her quick judgment. I will not let her feel that, for she probably did it unconsciously with her honesty. Heavens! Don't be afraid because of that. But for now I don't want any more letters from you, I'll forego such letters that take two weeks. Why should I be ashamed because Mechi is my sister?
