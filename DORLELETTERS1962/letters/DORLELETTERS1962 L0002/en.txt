Holland-America Line
R.M.S. Vancouver,
Feb. 21/62

Dear Ulla,

Thank you so very much for
your little letter. I just picked it up. The
mail arrived right in the middle of the sea.
We are quite cheerful and are keeping our
fingers crossed very tightly. Your letter made
me very happy, it had everything in it. You
are also quite something, always cheerful on
the horse, especially practical to be peeling
an apple, and on the side, "Please, four fruit
baskets, do not peel." And a lot less
time.
The two of them are always a source of joy
out goes the door closed, the bathroom
and everything is a huge affair.
When it's cold, the bathroom is cold,
heated. The night is downright grand, being able
to go to the loo at night and in the mornings
I always have a warm shower
and a bath in the evening. That will also
be nice in the summer, to take a cold
shower.
