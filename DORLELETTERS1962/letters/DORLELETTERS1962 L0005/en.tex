\noindent
Banff, Dec. 26/62

Dear Mutti,

Now I want to thank you very much for the lovely letter you sent me for Christmas a week ago, which I wasn't sure if I had received yet or not. As it turns out, I hadn't received one yet. I feel very well, though, that one isn't so stuffed full. People probably say the little birds are so full and content, the children laugh and are in diapers, so you have to take care of them a little. People complain and don't swaddle the little birds so they are full and content. The children are doing well. You can't give the children the first letter yet. Done out of carelessness.
