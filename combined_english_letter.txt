Dear Mother!
How are you? I want to
briefly write to you about the apple
prices at the wholesale market in
Frankfurt. We sold Cox apples
for 48 and 50 Pf per pound
to small retailers, and
there are still wormy ones among them.
The large one is, however, very
beautiful. Golden Delicious apples are selling
slowly, 25 Pf, but some
are worse than A. There are also
some for 32 Pf. Boskoops cost only
20 to 25 Pf, but they are not
particularly beautiful. We are not selling
any Boskoops yet. In Frankfurt
in the store, Cox apples are sold
for 70 Pf.
