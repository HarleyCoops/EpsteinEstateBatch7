Dear Dorli and Mucki!
Vancouver, July 31, 55

My journey home has just begun - westward. Despite terrible weather, not to say - lousy weather, I have had some wonderful days.
Just too short, as I have my ship booked for today.
When I woke up this morning, it was raining and it is still living and laughing today. We will carefully
the contributions and the orders
Your Gretel

Miss
M + J Hofman
53 Spadina Road
TORONTO, ONTARIO
