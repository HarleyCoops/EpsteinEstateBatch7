Mont Laurier, July 12, 55

Dear folks!
Now half of our vacation is already over. It was simply great. Every day we lay in the water for 2-3 hours, that is, on an air mattress. We don't cook, we eat cucumbers, canned peas with vinegar + oil, and bread, grapefruit and oranges and dried apricots. Sleeping in the car works great. We do it so that towards evening we wash and change at a lake, and when it gets dark, Inge already slips into her sleeping bag in the back and we drive to the next village, where we then sleep either by the first houses or at a gas station. Early in the morning at 5 o'clock I then crawl out of my sleeping bag and drive away before people arrive. Inge, of course, is still sleeping in the back. At the next lake, we then wash and have breakfast and then we drive a bit further until we are ready to swim at noon. We haven't planned our trip at all, that's precisely the best part. After we left Montreal, we went along the river to Quebec. Quebec is wonderful, an American city with city walls.
