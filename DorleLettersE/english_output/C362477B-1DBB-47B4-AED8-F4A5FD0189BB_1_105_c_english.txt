Monte, December 17, 1955

Dear Aunt!

On Christmas Eve, we came home at 6 o'clock after the Christmas Mass, but we immediately went out again at 10 o'clock after the children's gift-giving. At 12 o'clock, we came home again and made potato salad. On the 1st Christmas Day, we came home from the group, as we had already been at your place on Sunday evening. On the 2nd Christmas Day, we had a group evening, and it was very cozy. Imagine how full the hall was, absolutely crammed, with people even sitting on the benches at the end. The children sat in the first row. Here at the final service, it's a bit different, because the costume wasn't quite right.
