Nov. 11, 1941

Asked, it looked very good; said, it looked very good. Josef welcomed the Christmas carols that had been selected. She was not incapable. Ellen, Dagmar and Angelika vowed that evening to make a beautiful [setting for] the hearth, with one [of them] decorating the hearth. Immediately afterwards, Frede and I [performed] "Glory to God in every soul"; we had to sing and look, so that we stepped onto our mark every time. Then came the head teacher Mother and Sybille as Gabriel united, and every poem to a star and a new ne. The innkeepers: Joe as innkeeper. Apple with potatoes and my Gretel. Josef had a green robe reaching to the floor, and he was quite proud, and he is indeed very proud. Chair, where the crib scene was seen. We'd rather forget.
