No health insurance, not even the most expensive private insurance, covers the dentist, presumably because otherwise it would go bankrupt. The same applies to medication, if you are not in hospital. Now that it's Health Week, great care is once again taken to ensure everyone knows about vitamins and calories. I must say, much more attention is paid to this here, and fresh apples and oranges are eaten very much, as well as much more fresh vegetables than in Germany, which can be bought at cheap prices. Much more salad is eaten, especially coleslaw and lettuce, then raw carrots (whole ones) and raw celery, raw spinach (very good) and radishes! Cucumber slices (plain) and bell peppers. Salad here is mostly eaten with the hand, something fresh with every meal. Relatively few bananas are bought, although they are so cheap, but they don't contain many vitamins and make you fat.
